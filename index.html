Sinopsis

In Earth's future, a global crop blight and second Dust Bowl are slowly rendering the planet uninhabitable. Professor Brand (Michael Caine), a brilliant NASA physicist, is working on plans to save mankind by transporting Earth's population to a new home via a wormhole. But first, Brand must send former NASA pilot Cooper (Matthew McConaughey) and a team of researchers through the wormhole and across the galaxy to find out which of three planets could be mankind's new home.

REVIEW
A Great Film

Interstellar is a movie that I love and objectively it's very good. I like the concept of a not-so-distant future where Earth is losing its resources and is in crisis, where they must search for a world in which humanity can survive. Secrets, betrayals, this movie has it all. The fact that real physicists were behind the movie to ensure the calculations and formulas were accurate shows Nolan's effort and passion behind it. Interesting, entertaining, imaginative and realistic is how I would summarize it. Definitely a movie I would recommend.
